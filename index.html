How Spaced Repetition Works Here
When you mark a card ✅ Got It, its next review is scheduled further in the future (1 day → 3 days → 7 days → 14 days → 30 days). When you mark ❌ Review Again, it resets to 1 day. Cards due today appear in Due Today count and get prioritised in SRS Quiz. ⭐ Mastered = 5 correct reviews in a row with growing intervals.
